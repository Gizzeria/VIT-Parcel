Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

If you're going to the gate, post your schedule. If you need a parcel picked up, find someone in your block going to the gate and request them to pick it up for you.

How do I hand over my OTP?

You can provide the OTP or any verification details in the parcel description when you submit a request.

Is this free to use?

Yes! VIT Parcel is an entirely community-driven system built by and for the students.
